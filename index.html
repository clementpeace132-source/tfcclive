Our Mandate

The Fire-Christian Church is Afire for the Gospel. Our mandate is to spread the good news of Jesus Christ to all nations, making disciples and transforming lives through the power of the Holy Spirit. We believe in the transformative power of God's word and the active presence of the Holy Spirit in our daily lives.

We are committed to nurturing a community of believers who are passionate about God, dedicated to serving others, and equipped to impact their world for Christ. Join us as we pursue God's purpose and experience His fire!
